Crystal Game Collector

You are given a random number at the start of the game on top of the crystals.

There are four crystals, and each has a random value. The objective of the game is for you to collect as many crystals as needed to match the random number, otherwise you lose the game. By clicking on each crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
NOTE: The value of each crystal is hidden to you, until you click on it.

Each time the game reset, the game will change the value of the each crystal.
